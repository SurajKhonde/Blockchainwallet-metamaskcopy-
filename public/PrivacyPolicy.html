Privacy Policy

This is Hans Biotech’s personal information processing policy.

1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

The company processes personal information for the following purposes and will not use it for any other purposes. If the purpose of use changes, the company will take necessary measures, such as obtaining separate consent in accordance with Article 18 of the Personal Information Protection Act (hereinafter referred to as the “Act”).

  • Membership Management: Confirming membership registration, maintaining and managing membership qualifications, preventing unauthorized use of services, and handling various notices and complaints.
  • Service Provision: Providing wallet services such as deposits, withdrawals, and storage of virtual currency.

2. Items of Personal Information Collected

The company collects the following personal information items from users to process business smoothly according to the user's purpose of use:

  • When registering as a domestic member: Mobile phone number, name, date of birth, gender, CI, DI, email.
  • When registering as a member overseas (mobile phone): Mobile phone number, Name (Optional: Email).
  • When registering as a member overseas (email): Email, Name (Optional: Mobile phone number).
  • When consulting with the customer center: Mobile phone number, Email, Name.
  • Information automatically collected during service use: IP address, cookies, service usage history, device information.
  • When registering as a member under the age of 14: Those under the age of 14 cannot use the service, regardless of consent from their legal representative.

3. Processing and Retention Period of Personal Information

The company processes and retains personal information within the retention and use period stipulated by law or within the scope agreed upon by the user when collecting personal information. The retention period is as follows:

  • To prevent duplicate membership registration, your mobile phone number and email address will be retained for up to 1 year after withdrawal.
  • If preservation is required by other laws and regulations, it will be retained for the relevant period.
  • Records of contracts or cancellations of subscriptions: 5 years.
  • Records of payment and supply of goods: 5 years.
  • Records of consumer complaints or dispute resolution: 3 years.

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The company processes users' personal information only within the scope specified in '1. 6. Matters Concerning the Installation, Operation and Refusal of Automatic Personal Information Collection Devices

The company uses 'cookies' to store and periodically retrieve users' information. Cookies are small pieces of information sent to the user's computer browser by the server used to operate the website and are stored on the hard disk of the user's PC.

Users can choose whether to allow or reject cookie settings by adjusting options in their web browser.

7. Destruction of Personal Information, Procedures and Methods

The company destroys personal information without delay when it becomes unnecessary, such as when the retention period expires or the processing purpose is achieved.

  • Destruction procedure: Information is transferred to a separate DB and stored for a certain period according to internal policies and other relevant laws, then destroyed.
  • Method of destruction: Paper information is shredded or incinerated; electronic files are deleted using methods that make recovery impossible.

8. Measures to Ensure the Security of Personal Information

The company is taking the following measures to ensure the security of personal information:

  • Administrative measures: Internal management plan, regular employee training.
  • Technical measures: Access rights management, encryption, storage of access records, prevention of forgery and falsification.
  • Physical measures: Office and server room access control.

The company is not responsible for incidents due to user mistakes or basic internet risks. Users must manage their ID and password responsibly.

10. User Rights (Viewing, Correction, Deletion) and Exercise Methods

Users may exercise their rights to view, correct, delete, or suspend the processing of their personal information at any time. The rights can be exercised by contacting the Personal Information Protection Officer in writing, by phone, or by email, and the officer will take action without delay.

  • The rights can be exercised through an agent, such as the user's legal representative or authorized person.
  • Requests to view or suspend processing may be restricted or denied pursuant to Article 35, Paragraph 4 and Article 37, Paragraph 2 of the Personal Information Protection Act.
  • Requests for correction or deletion cannot be made if the personal information is explicitly stated as a collection target in other laws.
  • The company verifies the identity of the person making the request to ensure they are the user or a legitimate agent.
